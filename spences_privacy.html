Quick Summary: Spences helps you track your subscriptions and spending. We collect only the information necessary to provide our service, never sell your data, and use industry-standard security measures to protect your financial information.

1. Overview

Welcome to Spences, the subscription management app that helps you take control of your spending. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Spences, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your financial data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Subscription Data: Details about your subscriptions including service names, costs, billing cycles, and categories
  • Financial Information: Subscription costs and spending patterns (we do not store bank account numbers or credit card details)
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details like spending goals and preferences

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Analytics Data: App performance, crash reports, and usage statistics

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Optional Bank Connections: If you choose to connect your bank account, we receive transaction data through secure, encrypted connections with financial institutions
  • App Store Information: Purchase and download information from app stores

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Our Service: Track your subscriptions, calculate spending totals, and generate insights
  • Improve User Experience: Personalize the app, provide recommendations, and optimize features
  • Send Notifications: Alert you about upcoming renewals, spending limits, and important updates
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions, troubleshoot issues, and provide assistance
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns and improve app performance (using aggregated, anonymized data)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Never Sell Your Data: Spences does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our app, including:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage services
  • Analytics and crash reporting services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Payment processing services

These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request, or to:

  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
  • Prevent fraud or other illegal activities
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Connections: All communications use HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Data stored in SOC 2 compliant data centers

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication when available.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for up to 30 days after account deletion
  • Subscription Data: Retained to provide historical insights and analytics
  • Usage Data: Typically retained for 24 months for analytics and improvement purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Export your subscription data in a portable format

7.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update your account information within the app
  • Correct inaccurate subscription data

7.3 Deletion

  • Delete individual subscriptions or data entries
  • Request complete account deletion

7.4 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Manage notification settings within the app
  • Unsubscribe from emails using the link provided

7.5 Data Processing Restrictions

  • Object to certain data processing activities
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances

8. Children's Privacy

Spences is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

Parents and guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us immediately.

9. International Users

Spences is operated from the United States. If you use our service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions for certain countries
  • Other legally recognized transfer mechanisms

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email
  • We will provide at least 30 days' notice for significant changes
  • Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Thank you for trusting Spences with your financial data. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security to help you take control of your subscriptions and spending.
