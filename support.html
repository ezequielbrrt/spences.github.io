Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new subscription to track? +
To add a subscription, tap the "+" button in the app, enter the service name, cost, and billing frequency. You can also categorize it (streaming, productivity, etc.) and set up renewal reminders.
Can I connect my bank account to automatically track subscriptions? +
No! Spences don't save your banking credentials.
